How to Use

  1. Upload Images: Use "Upload Self Image" and "Upload Clothing/Accessory" to select your photos.
  2. Position Yourself so your full body or torso is clearly visible for the best outfit preview.
  3. Preview Your Photos to make sure lighting and angles are ideal. Retake if needed.
  4. Tap "Continue" to proceed to the virtual try-on page with your uploaded images.
  5. Explore Styles by browsing, mixing, and matching outfits to find your perfect look.
  6. Save & Share your favorite looks with friends or save them to your profile.

Key Features

  • AI-Powered Try-On: Instantly see how clothes fit and look on your uploaded photo.
  • Wide Variety of Styles: Explore trendy clothing and accessories from our extensive catalog.
  • Easy Mix & Match: Combine tops, bottoms, and accessories to create unique outfits.
  • Photo Management: Retake, replace, or continue with your photos at any time.
  • Save & Share: Keep your favorite outfits or share them with friends for feedback.
  • User-Friendly Interface: Simple navigation makes virtual styling fun and easy.
