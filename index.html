How to Use

  1. Upload Your Images: Click the buttons above to upload a photo of yourself and a clothing item or accessory.
  2. Position Yourself: Ensure your full body or torso is visible in your photo.
  3. Preview Your Photos: Both images will be displayed above for confirmation.
  4. Tap "Continue": Proceed to the virtual try-on page with your uploaded photos.
  5. Explore Styles: Mix and match outfits to find your perfect look.
  6. Save & Share: Keep your favorite outfits or share them with friends.
