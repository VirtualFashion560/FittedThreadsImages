How to Use

  1. Upload Images: Choose a photo of yourself and a clothing item to see how it looks on you.
  2. Position Clearly: Make sure your body and the clothing item are fully visible for the best result.
  3. Preview: Check that both images look correct. Use "Retake" to replace either photo if needed.
  4. Tap "Continue": Proceed to the virtual try-on page with your uploaded photos.
  5. Explore Styles: Mix and match outfits to find your perfect look.
  6. Save & Share: Keep your favorite looks or share with friends for feedback.

Key Features

  • AI-Powered Try-On: Instantly see how clothes fit and look on your uploaded photo.
  • Wide Variety of Styles: Explore trendy clothing and accessories from our extensive catalog.
  • Easy Mix & Match: Combine tops, bottoms, and accessories to create unique outfits.
  • Photo Management: Retake, replace, or continue with your photos at any time.
  • Save & Share: Keep your favorite outfits or share them with friends for feedback.
