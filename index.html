How to Use

  1. Upload your photo and clothing item to preview how the outfit will look on you.
  2. Position Yourself & Clothing Clearly so AI can generate an accurate try-on.
  3. Preview Both Images side by side to ensure quality and alignment.
  4. Retake any image if needed.
  5. Tap "Continue" to go to the AI try-on page.
  6. Explore Styles and save/share your favorite looks.
