How to Use

  1. Tap the + button (top-right) in the app to upload or take a photo of yourself or a clothing item.
  2. Our system uploads the image securely and sends it to the AI processor for virtual try-on generation.
  3. When processing completes you’ll see realistic outfit overlays and suggested styles. Save or share the looks you love.
  4. If you want a different look, upload another photo or mix & match items from the catalog.

Key Features

  • AI-powered virtual try-on: See garments rendered on your photo to preview fit and style.
  • Extensive catalog: Browse curated trends and swap items to build outfits fast.
  • Mix & match: Combine tops, bottoms, and accessories to experiment with looks.
  • Save & share: Keep favorite outfits or share looks with friends for feedback.
  • Privacy-focused: Images are processed securely and retained only as needed (details in Privacy Policy).
