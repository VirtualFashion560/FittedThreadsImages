How to Use

  1. Tap "Let’s Get Started" to upload your own photo and a clothing or accessory photo.
  2. Position Yourself in your photo so your full body or torso is clearly visible.
  3. Preview Both Photos to make sure lighting and angles are ideal. Retake if needed.
  4. Tap "Continue" to go to the try-on page where the AI will combine them.
  5. Explore Styles by mixing clothing pieces, accessories, and outfit combinations.
  6. Save & Share your favorite AI-generated outfits with friends.

Key Features

  • AI-Powered Try-On: See how any clothing item looks on you instantly.
  • Dual Photo Upload: Upload yourself and a clothing/accessory item for realistic previews.
  • Easy Mix & Match: Test multiple items without changing clothes in real life.
  • Photo Management: Retake, replace, or update either photo at any time.
  • Save & Share: Keep your favorite combinations or share them for feedback.
  • Beautiful Autumn Theme: Warm, inviting design to enhance your try-on experience.
