How to Use

  1. Upload Two Photos: One of yourself and one of the clothing/accessory you want to try on.
  2. Check Your Previews: Make sure both images are clear and well-lit. Use "Retake" if needed.
  3. Tap "Continue": See AI-powered results in the try-on page.
  4. Explore Styles: Mix and match clothing, accessories, and colors.
  5. Save & Share: Share your virtual outfits or save them for later.
