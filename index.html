How to Use

  1. Upload Your Photos: Use the buttons above to upload a self image and a clothing item.
  2. Preview Your Photos: Make sure your images look correct. Use Retake if needed.
  3. Tap "Continue" to proceed to the virtual try-on page with your uploaded photos.
  4. Explore Styles: Mix and match outfits or select items from our catalog.
  5. Save & Share: Keep your favorite looks or share with friends.

  • Photo Management: Retake, replace, or continue with your photos at any time.
