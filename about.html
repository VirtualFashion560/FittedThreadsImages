About Fitted Threads

Fitted Threads was created to make styling simple, fun, and accessible. We combine machine learning and fashion expertise to let you preview outfits on your own photos — so you can discover looks that truly fit your style and life.

Our vision

We believe trying on clothes should be effortless. Our goal is to reduce guesswork, boost confidence, and help everyone find a style that feels personal.

What we build

  • High-quality AI virtual try-ons that respect proportion and lighting
  • A curated catalog of trend-forward pieces
  • Simple tools for saving and sharing your looks

The team

We’re a small team of designers, engineers, and stylists passionate about fashion + tech. If you’d like to collaborate, support, or share feedback — please get in touch via Contact Us.
