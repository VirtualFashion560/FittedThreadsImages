Privacy Policy

Last updated: August 2025

Overview

Fitted Threads values your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your options.

What we collect

  • Uploaded images: When you use the virtual try-on, you upload photos (selfies or clothing items) so the AI can generate results.
  • Metadata: Timestamps and minimal usage data (e.g., which features are used) to improve the product.
  • Optional contact info: If you contact support we may collect your email and message.

How we use your data

We use uploaded images purely to run AI models and provide the try-on experience. We may also use aggregated usage data to improve our models and app features.

Third-party services

We rely on a few trusted providers to deliver features:

  • Cloudinary (or similar): image storage and CDN for fast uploads and downloads.
  • Hosting & analytics: services to operate and monitor the app.
  • Google Sheets / Zapier / Backend: optional integrations you may see during early workflows to store and manage uploads.

Data retention and deletion

We retain uploaded images only as long as necessary to provide and improve the service. If you want your image or account data deleted, contact us at Contact Us or email support@fittedthreads.com and we will remove it promptly.

Security

We encrypt data in transit (HTTPS) and follow industry practices to protect data at rest. No system is perfectly secure — if we discover a data incident we will notify affected users and authorities as appropriate.

Your choices

You can delete your images and data, opt out of analytics, or request a copy of your data. Contact us to exercise these rights.

If you have questions, please visit Contact Us.
